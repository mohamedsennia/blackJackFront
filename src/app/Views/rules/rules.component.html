BlackJack Rules

  1. Objective of the Game:

    The goal is to have a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's hand without exceeding 21.

  2. Card Values:

    • Number cards (2-10): Face value.
    • Face cards (King, Queen, Jack): Worth 10 points.
    • Ace: Worth 1 or 11 points, depending on what benefits the hand.

  3. Gameplay:

    - Each round the player can choose one of two options:
    • Draw a new card
    • Fold, this means he thinks the hand he has at the moment is his best chance of winning

  4. Winning Conditions:

    • If the player reach 21 points first
    • If the other player gets busted (get more points thant 21)
    • If both players fold, the player with more points wins
